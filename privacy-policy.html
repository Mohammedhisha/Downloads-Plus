Privacy Policy for Downloads Plus

Last Updated: April 8, 2026

1. Introduction

Downloads Plus is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data when you use our Chrome Extension.

2. Data We Access

Downloads Plus requests the following permissions:

  • downloads: To read your download history including file names, URLs, sizes, and states, and to allow pausing, resuming, cancelling, or removing downloads.
  • tabs: To detect when you visit chrome://downloads and redirect you to the enhanced dashboard.
  • storage: To save your UI preferences locally on your device.

3. Data Storage and Transmission

All data remains on your device. Downloads Plus does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information, download history, or browsing data on external servers. All processing is done locally within your browser. We do not use analytics or third-party trackers.

4. Local Storage

The extension uses Chrome's storage API exclusively to save your UI preferences locally. This data never leaves your browser.

5. Third-Party Libraries

All third-party libraries are bundled locally within the extension package. No external scripts or resources are loaded at runtime.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above.

7. Contact Us

For questions, please contact us through the Chrome Web Store support page for the extension.
